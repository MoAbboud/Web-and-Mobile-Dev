Why dogs?

and why are they the best.

  1. They are the best companion.
  2. They are loyal.
  3. They are cuddly.
  4. They are playful.

Dogs offer a lot of great benefits for humans.

  1. They lower our stress levels.
    Being able to come home every day to the sight of a wagging tail in a windmill of excitement and happiness is bound to make all that stress and tiredness go away.

  2. They are the best coworkers.
    Dogs have been doing an amazing job in any position they are placed. They make great police dogs, firefighting dogs, and therapy and support dogs.

  3. They help humans sleep better.
    Studies have shown that dogs offer a sense of security and comfort to humans allowing them to sleep better and comfortably.

  4. They keep us healthy.
    Having a dog carries a huge sense of responsibility to maintain and ensure their comfort and well-being including taking them out for walks which as a result will provide exercise for both the dog and the human.
